Information: Customization Guidelines


Welcome to the customization page! Here, you can upload multiple images such as your logo, design
ideas, or even finished designs. Next to the upload button, you'll find a text box where you can
provide additional instructions or details about your design preferences.
For example, you might write: "Please add my store hours at the bottom (7:00 AM to 11:00 PM) and
include my tagline: 'Quality You Can Trust.'"


Please note that while this page allows you to share your ideas and upload materials, it doesn’t offer
tools for you to create the design yourself. Instead, our skilled in-house designers will take your
provided images and text, understand your requirements, and craft the perfect design for you.
Once your order is completed, we will email you the finalized design within 3-5 business days. We
offer up to three revisions to ensure the design meets your approval.
Feel free to provide as much detail as possible so we can deliver a design that meets your
expectations!
