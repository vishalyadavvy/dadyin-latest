About Us

At Dayana Polyplast INC, we specialize in importing high-quality custom and generic packaging products and liner solutions. With over 70 years of experience, we proudly serve retailers such as pharmacies, supermarkets, discount stores, dollar stores, and liquor stores.

Our streamlined selection of products and competitive pricing have made us a trusted partner in the packaging industry. We offer a wide range of tote bags in materials like jute, cotton, woven, non-woven, PE, LDPE, and paper. Our bags come with options such as W-cut, D-cut, and separate handles.

Additionally, we provide customizable liner bags in various formats—rolls, sheets, or bags—to meet the needs of the food, retail, and construction industries.

Our partnerships with over 35 global manufacturers enable us to serve major retailers like C-Town Supermarkets, Dollar General, and One Dollar Zone. We also proudly support food industry leaders like Lafe and Empanada King, and construction giants such as Johns Manville and Zoria Scaffolding.

Whether you're in retail, food, or construction, Dayana Polyplast is here to help with your packaging needs.
